About ECOMED

“We don’t just study biodiversity
— we design the future of living systems.”

ECOMED (www.eco-med.gr), established in 2004, provides comprehensive services in the fields of Environment, Energy Transition & Climate, Sustainable Development, Biodiversity, Environmental Training, and Licensing. The company is dedicated to minimizing the environmental, social, and energy impacts of businesses, industries, construction sites, infrastructure projects, cultural events, and more by designing tailored sustainable solutions.

ECOMED E.E. also possesses extensive expertise in delivering environmental training to employees. The organization's strengths lie in its team of highly skilled professionals and its consistently satisfied, loyal client base.

Why we’re doing this

Biodiversity is not just about endangered species — it's about the systems that keep life, business, and society functioning. And yet, most people don't understand what biodiversity really means — or why it matters to them.

As ECOMED transitions into a consultancy centered on biodiversity intelligence, we believe the most ethical and impactful move is to start with education, not obligation. These webinars are designed to open minds, spark meaningful dialogue, and invite partnerships based on shared values — not pressure.
